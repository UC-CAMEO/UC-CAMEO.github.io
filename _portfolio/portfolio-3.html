LinaBell made her debut at Shanghai Disneyland on September 29, 2021, being introduced on social media two weeks earlier on the 16th. LinaBell also made her debut at Tokyo DisneySea on September 8, 2022 and at Hong Kong Disneyland on September 9, 2022. LinaBell made a surprise appearance at the 2022 D23 Expo, at the presentation for Disney Parks, Experiences and Products and at the Duffy and Friends panel alongside Duffy.
