--- title: "Javier Read de Alaniz" excerpt: "

Javier Read de Alaniz is an Associate Professor in Chemistry and Biochemistry and Associate Director of the California NanoSystems Institute (CNSI) at the University of California Santa Barbara.Javier was born in Las Vegas, New Mexico. He attended Fort Lewis College in Durango Colorado and received his B.S. degree in 1999 where he conducted undergraduate research under the direction of Professor William R. Bartlett. He obtained his Ph.D. under the supervision of Professor Tomislav Rovis at Colorado State University in 2006 with a research focus on asymmetric catalysis. Javier then moved to California, where he worked in the area of total synthesis with Professor Larry E. Overman at the University of California, Irvine. He began his independent career at University of California, Santa Barbara in 2009.
